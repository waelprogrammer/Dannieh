ABOUT AL-DANNIEH

Discover the rich history and natural beauty of our special region in Northern Lebanon

A Land of Mountains and Tradition

Al-Dannieh is a picturesque district located in the northern part of Lebanon, nestled within the stunning mountain ranges of the country. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and dense pine forests, the region offers visitors a unique glimpse into traditional Lebanese village life.

With its moderate climate and rich biodiversity, Al-Dannieh has been inhabited since ancient times. The region is home to numerous archaeological sites, historic monuments, and traditional villages that preserve Lebanon's cultural heritage.

Today, Al-Dannieh is known for its agricultural products, particularly olive oil, fruits, and vegetables, as well as for its emerging eco-tourism opportunities that allow visitors to connect with nature and experience authentic Lebanese hospitality.
